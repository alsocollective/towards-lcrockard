A Stand In For
the Thing

A Project by
Liam Crockard
& Towards

The screen flickers as the camera tries to focus. Items suddenly begin to fill the frame. A cast away gesture, the clearing of the slate. Viewed through this lens, the actions appear both familiar and foreign at once. The scale imbues them with a sensuality, a level of detail and texture that is made possible only through a perfectly flat surface. The forms shift and develop over time. In doing so, they take on the role of props. They act as stand ins, signifiers that there is some sort of artistic production happening elsewhere, just slightly off screen.
